Hack for Farming - East Africa

Bongo la Zaraa


Find Out More

Join us in Nairobi, September 26-28

Agriculture organizations across East Africa are working hard to help smallholder farmers adapt to changing conditions and take advantage of new technologies. But these organizations face numerous challenges in collecting, managing, and communicating their data effectively. Our hackathon will team up experts from these organizations with local programmers in an intensive, 48-hour effort to brainstorm solutions to common problems.

Scroll down to learn how to participate, and watch this space for further details as they become available.
