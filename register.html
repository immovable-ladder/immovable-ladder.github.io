How to Register

Updated on June 1, 2015

To register for the Hackathon you must fill out this form: Survey Monkey

The linked form will ask you for details of your background and expertise that will help us build teams with compatible interests, languages, and technical skills. Once you have filled out the form, we will be in touch with you within a few days. Remember, even if you do not feel ready to participate in a team, we would welcome you to:

Attend our Kick-Off event

You'll hear our keynote speaker give insight into how technology can advance East African agriculture, and have a chance to network with hacking teams, agriculture experts, and tech-minded folks the Friday before the Hackathon kicks off.

Attend the Team Presentations

At the end of the Hackathon on Sunday, teams will prepare brief presentations of their solutions, and speak to a panel of judges who will determine winners and award the prizes.

Mentor an organization

Many of the agricultural organizations attending will have limited hackathon experience, and could use guidance from experienced coders. Let us know if you'd like to be a point of contact for organizations trying to develop their technological capacity and know-how.

Propose a challenge

Do you work in agriculture in East Africa? Get in touch with us to talk about the persistent data challenges you face. We hope to design a challenge that has the potential to serve a broad community of people who are working toward a more productive future for East African agriculture.
